Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how TackPath ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our courier dispatch and delivery tracking services, including our website, customer portal, driver application, and SMS notifications.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including your name, phone number, delivery address, and order details when you place or receive a delivery through TackPath.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill delivery orders
  • To send order confirmations, tracking links, and delivery status updates
  • To coordinate between customers, drivers, and dispatchers
  • To improve our services and customer experience

SMS Communications

Your mobile phone number will never be shared or sold to any third party or affiliate for marketing or promotional purposes. Phone numbers collected for SMS notifications are used solely to send delivery-related messages as described in this policy.

Message frequency varies based on your delivery activity. You may receive messages related to order confirmation, dispatch updates, and delivery completion. Message and data rates may apply.

You can opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. For help, reply HELP or contact us using the information below.

Information Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes. Information may be shared with drivers and dispatchers solely to complete your delivery.

Data Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us through tackpath.com.
