How it Works

From order to delivered in three steps.

TackPath handles the entire delivery lifecycle. Your team focuses on the deliveries. The platform handles everything else.

01
Order placed
Your customer places a delivery order through the TackPath customer portal. Sprint for single deliveries. Surge for multi-stop routes via manifest upload. The Intake Agent validates the order instantly.
→
02
Driver dispatched
Available drivers are notified immediately on the TackPath driver app. First to accept gets the job. They navigate stop by stop, scan packages, and collect proof of delivery at every door.
→
03
Delivered and billed
The final stop is confirmed with POD. Customer receives automatic delivery confirmation. The Billing Agent prepares the invoice. You approve and send. The whole loop closes without a single phone call.

AI Agents

Intelligence built into every operation.

TackPath's AI agents run continuously in the background -- monitoring, communicating, analyzing, and alerting. The more your operation runs, the sharper they get.

Operations Coordinator (OCA)
Watches every active job every 60 seconds. Detects stalled deliveries, unassigned jobs, and SLA risks before they become failures. Creates work items for your approval.
⬤ LIVE
Communication Agent
Sends automatic delivery updates to customers at every status change. Driver assigned, picked up, approaching, delivered. Customers always know where their package is.
⬤ LIVE
Intake Agent
Validates every new order the moment it arrives. Checks addresses, pricing, package counts. Flags issues before dispatch so drivers never head to the wrong location.
⬤ LIVE
Billing Agent
Reviews completed deliveries, verifies proof of delivery exists, and prepares invoice line items for owner approval. Billing runs itself at the end of every day.
⬤ LIVE
Revenue Intelligence Agent (RIA)
Analyzes every delivered job and tells you which clients are profitable, which routes are losing money, and where to raise prices. Business intelligence without a CFO.
⬤ LIVE
Dispatch Agent (SmartPath)
Assigns the optimal driver based on real-time location and traffic. Calculates live ETAs. Reroutes automatically when conditions change on the road.
